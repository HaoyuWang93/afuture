About


This ongoing project (which will always be ongoing, including this 'about' section) situates upon the shared cultural pursuit of social imagination between architectural discourse and SF (science fiction). It aims to incorporate architecture and urban design not only as hypothetical stimuli to alternative future conditions but also as narrative devices for an open-ended social imagination of fears and wonders.

While architects have been producing 'THE future' with countless drawings and models, I would rather categorize my provocations as 'another' for wishing a better version to happen in reality.
