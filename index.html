Another Future

What if the COVID pandemic extend for decades?
What if technologies of remote work reach maximum capacities?

Another Future is an open-ended architectural fiction that accommodates and proceeds with a series of loosely linked stories in a post-2020 world.

I will keep posting new stories and events to the timeline.
Tip: you may find your mobile screen a better fit for panoramic scenes in the stories.

[2020] COVID-19 Outbreak

COVID-19 OUTBREAK

×

The pandemic era begins with COVID-19 sweeping across the globe,
urban routines of working/ schooling/ shopping/ recreation/ social life turn into remote forms under social distancing requirements;

[2021-] COVID Normalization

COVID NORMALIZATION

×

The continuous fights between COVID mutations and public health measures normalize social distancing practice, regulated infection testings, seasonal vaccination reinforcements, and periodical lockdowns in most urban societies;

[2030-] 5.0 Technologies

5.0 TECHNOLOGIES

×

The COVID transitions of social production stimulate economic and political investments on 5.0 Infrastructures:
- 5G Cellular Network for data transmission;
- Industry 5.0 for unmanned production;
- Lv. 5 Mobility for material flows;

[-2035-] Digital Gaps

DIGITAL GAPS

×

Digital production thrives across diverse industries and services under the dual impacts of COVID developments and 5.0 Technologies, amplifying the social gaps between the emerging populations of full-time digital workers and their onsite counterparts (or commuter populations);

While unmanned production sites occupy less-inhabitable lands and waters with 5.0-equipped machinaries to maximize their productivities toward energy thresholds, their limited hiring slots leave most population behind to industrial reservations by political acts with unstable employment under COVID impacts, let alone the rapidly increasing number of unemployed who live on rations;

Digital economies also challenge the relationships between workforce and residency, giving birth to United Digital Council (UDC) as an political alliance to regulate cross-border productions, consumptions, jurisdictions, and taxations;

[-2040-] Drifting Cities

DRIFTING CITIES

×
The resumed industrial activities under 5.0 Technologies accelerate global warming progress, causing not only sea-level-rises to flood coastal cities earlier than expected but also intensive COVID mutations as combined with the once-frozen pathogens from melted polar ice.

The recurring disasters of extreme climates, sea-rises, COVID lockdowns, and their accompanied social disorders increasingly destabilize urban societies and create nomadic populations on constant escapes.

[STORY] HOME PORT

HOMEPORT

HOME PORT

×

'...so why did you move out of campus?'

This recurring question comes from not only my digital fellows but also my social-distanced neighbors here in the submerged South Boston. As the Omega variant calls another victory of COVID against vaccines, campus towers seem to be the only places free from the next inevitable lockdowns. Campus Group probably won't believe that their vertical city concept would win this year for controlled access rather than being 'unsinkable' as they initially advertised to sea-rise victims.

It is true that as digital workers, our expectations for physical beings lie on the leisure side. So we support controlled access to campus and any tracking measures inside, as long as we can have endless parties while people outside struggle against COVID for their onsite jobs. On the other hand, I somehow smelt another level of lockdown framed by the tower's COVID-PASS infrastructure when watching service drones circled around for portal scanning sequence and hologram ads redirecting my walk to a 'healthier' lunch spot. My incremental fears for the next PASS updates culminated with my escape from campus and eventually led me to South Boston, where I can live untrackable under masks and no longer be socially distanced from the entire city.

However, this life is about to change when DPH announces the city's next response to the upcoming Omega...
